# Product Guidelines: assistantphd.com

## Visual Identity
- **Modern & Minimalist:** The website should feature clean lines, ample white space, and a focus on essential elements. This aesthetic conveys a sense of professional precision and clarity.

## Prose & Tone
- **Expert & Authoritative:** Use precise and confident language to convey deep knowledge and high-level competence.
- **Efficient & Direct:** Copy should be concise, focusing on saving the user time with clear calls to action.

## Brand Messaging
- **Modern Efficiency:** Focus on the use of cutting-edge tools and highly efficient workflows to deliver superior results.
- **Precision Support:** Position the service as a sophisticated solution for complex administrative or specialized needs.
