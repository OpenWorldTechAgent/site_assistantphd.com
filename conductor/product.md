# Product Definition: assistantphd.com

## Initial Concept
This repo will be used for a website for modern assistantphd.com. This site should include sections for:
- main page, about us, services, appointment scheduling, contacts

## Target Audience
The primary audience for assistantphd.com includes:
- **Small Business Owners & Independent Consultants:** Individuals running their own ventures who need high-level administrative or specialized support.
- **Busy Executives:** High-level professionals looking for efficient, expert assistance to manage their workloads.

## Primary Goals
The website aims to enable visitors to:
- **Schedule Consultations:** Seamlessly book service appointments or consultations.
- **Explore Services:** Gain a clear understanding of the specific services and packages offered.
- **Engage with the Brand:** Understand the unique value proposition and story behind "Assistant PhD."

## Key Features
- **Integrated Appointment Booking:** A functional system for scheduling meetings directly on the site.
- **Service Catalog:** A comprehensive list of services provided, detailing what each entails.
- **Dynamic Contact Form:** A dedicated section for inquiries, allowing potential clients to reach out with specific needs.
